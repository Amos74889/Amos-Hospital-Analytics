Model Comparison — MAE & RMSE
Lower values = better accuracy · Random Forest vs ARIMA
🌲 When to trust Random Forest
Best for pattern recognition across multiple disease types. Reliable when R² is above 85%. Use for identifying which wards and months are most at risk.
📈 When to trust ARIMA
Best for time-series forecasting — predicting next month's admissions. Requires 6+ months of data. Ideal for advance medicine ordering and staff scheduling.
